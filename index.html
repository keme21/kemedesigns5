My Beliefs

I like to consider myself an Expert-Learner, I have seen that in life, nobody knows everything no matter how skilled you are, or even if you are at the top of your game there is always someone out there who knows something you don’t, that is why I always take the opportunity to learn from people whenever I’m given the opportunity to.

I believe that Design is the foundation for anything that functions, and it is a fundamental part of everything in life. Degign must be seamless and the user should always be able to know what to do without reading some sort of manual or tutorial. The Father of Design- Don Norman once said “A door that tells you how to open it is bad, when you look at something you should just be able to tell what to do, and this can be applied to a lot of things in life”- That is currently my favorite quote, and it has been guiding me lately on my designs
